## State of Skills Newsletter

The **State of Skills** newsletter is NDZ's primary weekly publication that delivers insights on:

- Digital skills development and career growth
- Learning strategies and mindset shifts
- Industry trends and opportunities
- Practical advice for skill mastery

## How to Stay Informed

There are multiple ways to keep up with NDZ updates:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Follow on Social Media">
    Follow **@NDZAcademy** on Twitter, YouTube, and other platforms for real-time updates and announcements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe to Newsletter">
    Get the State of Skills newsletter delivered to your inbox weekly with insights on digital skills and career growth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Weekly Roundups">
    Visit the [weekly roundups page](/updates/weekly-roundups) for summaries of new articles, newsletters, and platform updates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Community">
    Engage with the NDZ community forum at [ndz.ng](https://ndz.ng) to participate in discussions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Update Frequency

- **Weekly Roundups**: Published regularly covering platform activities from the previous week
