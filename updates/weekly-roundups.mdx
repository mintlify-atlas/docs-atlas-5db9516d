Each weekly or monthly roundup typically includes:

## Key Themes in Roundups

NDZ roundups consistently cover:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Skill Development Resources">
    Articles highlighting top YouTube channels, online platforms, podcasts, and other learning resources for various digital skills including data science, copywriting, UI/UX design, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Career Opportunities">
    Guides on freelance opportunities, part-time jobs, and monetization strategies for students and professionals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mindset and Strategy">
    State of Skills newsletter covering learning strategies, goal setting, work decisions, and personal development approaches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Community Engagement">
    Regular reminders to follow @NDZAcademy on social media and participate in the NDZ community forum.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Publication Schedule

- **Weekly Roundups**: Typically published for specific week ranges (e.g., "September 9th - 13th")
- **Monthly Roundups**: Comprehensive summaries published at the end of each month
- **Tag**: Posts are tagged with "Last week on NDZ" or "Last month on NDZ"

## Accessing Roundups

Roundup posts are published on the main NDZ blog and can be accessed through:

- The blog section at [ndz.ng](https://ndz.ng)
- Social media announcements on @NDZAcademy
- State of Skills newsletter references

Each roundup includes direct links to all mentioned articles and newsletters for easy access.
