## What We DON'T Cover

<Warning>
  These topics don't align with NDZ's philosophy and won't be published:
</Warning>

- **Get-rich-quick schemes** or "done-for-you" money systems
- **Cult-like thinking** or one-size-fits-all solutions
- **Content for complainers** or victim mindset reinforcement
- **Expensive tools/systems** that create barriers to action
- **Trendy hacks** that won't be relevant in 6 months
- **Motivational fluff** without actionable frameworks

## How to Choose Your Topic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with a question you're wrestling with">
    The best NDZ articles come from real struggles. What are you trying to figure out?
  </Step>

  <Step title="Look for the tension">
    Where is conventional wisdom contradictory? (Example: "Focus on one thing" vs "Multiple income streams")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test against Lindy">
    Will this topic matter in 5 years? Or is it tied to a current trend?
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask: Is this actionable?">
    Can you provide a framework, practice, or mental model readers can use?
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check: Does this fit our audience?">
    Is this relevant to remote workers, freelancers, skill-builders, or people fighting procrastination?
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  When in doubt, ask yourself: "Would this help someone become a ruthless executor who's focused on getting results?"
</Tip>
