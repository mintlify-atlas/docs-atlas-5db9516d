## No Distraction Zone

NDZ Journal is the beginner's guide and knowledge base for the **No Distraction Zone** - Nigeria's first focus community and a community-first education platform for remote workers and digital creators.

Whether you're struggling with procrastination, overthinking, or lack of motivation, NDZ provides the resources, community, and guidance to help you become a better, more focused version of yourself.

<Note>
  This journal serves as your comprehensive guide to navigating the NDZ community, accessing resources, and making the most of your membership.
</Note>

## Philosophy: Less is More

NDZ adopts a **minimalist philosophy** in words and actions to achieve clear-cut focus in work and life. We celebrate the process as much as we do the results, teaching what works and what doesn't through honest, practical guidance.
